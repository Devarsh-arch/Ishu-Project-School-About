About My School

My school is one of the best and most reputed school of this city. My school offers education right from kindergarten to 12th standard. My school is equipped with all sorts of modern day facility to enchance education. My school has a play ground, an assembly hall, library, and activity lab.

My school offers teaching in English and Gujarati medium and is affliated to Gujarat Secondary Education Board. The teachers in my school make learning easy and interesting by providing presentations and demonstrations.
